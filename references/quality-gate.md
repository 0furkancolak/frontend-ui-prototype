# Prototype quality gate

A prototype is not complete until it has been rendered and revised.

## Scorecard — 100 points

- Primary task/value clarity: 20
- Information and visual hierarchy: 15
- Product truth and credibility: 15
- Design-system consistency: 15
- Visual evidence/illustration quality: 10
- Interaction completeness: 10
- Responsive behavior: 10
- Accessibility fundamentals: 5

Target: 85 or higher.

## Browser checks

- 1440 × 900
- 1024 × 768
- 390 × 844

Check overflow, clipping, image loading, mobile order, navigation, CTA behavior, focus visibility, reduced motion, and console output.

## Mandatory revision

After the first screenshot pass, identify the three highest-impact weaknesses and fix them. Re-render before handoff.
