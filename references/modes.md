# Prototype modes

## New concept

Research the market category and comparable workflows. Build the smallest page or interaction that makes the idea understandable.

## Existing app to landing page

Inspect real routes, screenshots, language, roles, and visual tokens. Lead with product evidence. Operational screens may be reframed, cropped, sequenced, or placed into a composed device/browser shell, but should remain truthful.

## Existing app UI prototype

Preserve product conventions. The prototype may challenge one major interaction or hierarchy decision at a time.

## Reference-led original

Create a reference matrix separating product pattern, composition, and art direction. Adapt principles, not identity.
