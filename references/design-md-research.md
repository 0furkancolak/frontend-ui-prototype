# DESIGN.md research

Use the official Google specification as the structural authority. Community libraries are discovery sources, not automatic truth.

## Required research output

For each of three candidates, capture:

- URL/source and name
- category tags
- visual thesis
- audience fit
- typography system
- color behavior
- shape/elevation behavior
- page composition implications
- screenshot/photo/illustration strategy
- major risk

## Candidate differentiation

Candidates must differ in composition and evidence model. Examples:

- product-led editorial
- human-centered photographic
- illustration/diagram-led explanatory

Do not present three nearly identical light SaaS systems.

## Adopted file

Write a product-specific file following the official section order:

1. Overview
2. Colors
3. Typography
4. Layout
5. Elevation & Depth
6. Shapes
7. Components
8. Do's and Don'ts
9. Imagery and Illustration
10. Motion
11. Research provenance

Tokens in YAML frontmatter are normative. Prose explains intent.
